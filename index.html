About Us


Since its inception in 2004, Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT), Kanpur has been a frontrunner in the field of education with numerous achievements to its name. PSIT believes in strengthening the foundation of the students. Academic research based on rigorous pedagogy equips our students to face challenges in the real world. The institute prepares students to be ethical leaders in a diverse and complex world. PSIT offers various courses in Engineering, Business Management and Computer Applications. All departments, with their dynamic community of students and distinguished faculty, offer premium undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
